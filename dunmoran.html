Dunmoran Strand, Co. Sligo

Dunmoran Strand is a sheltered beach break that has quite reliable surf and can work at any time of the year. Ideal winds are from the south. Great place to walk along, surf and bathe, enjoy the sunset, dog walking, a camp for a night. This beach got Green Coast Award for 2018. (Green Coast Award is given for excellent water quality, great environment standard, good management, and involvement of community). Dunmoran is go to spot for Sligo surfers. It picks up more swell than anywhere around. Surfable on the tiny north wind swells and one of the few places on this north facing coast that will catch a bit of south west swell. Multiple peaks depending on swell, swell direction, sand placement and tide. Find out more here
